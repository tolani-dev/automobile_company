AUTO WORKSHOP

NasTECH AutoWorkshop is a specialist in automotive repair and maintenance services. The company offers best-in-class services using fully computerized processes and genuine spare parts.

Our team has been trained to cater to the specific needs of each of the vehicle brands we specialize in: Mercedes-Benz, Toyota, Higer and Hyundai.

Our workshops are equipped with the most recent and sophisticated diagnostic equipment for each vehicle brand, with over 50 work Bays, a Body Section, Alignment and Balancing Sections, as well as highly-trained and experienced mechanical staff.
